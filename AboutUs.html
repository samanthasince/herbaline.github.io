ABOUT US

How did it all started?

As a kid, I love drinking tea made by my mother every morning. It made me feel fresh and good. Tea have always been part of my life and I wanted to share my passion and love for tea with other people. So I came up with an idea of opening a tea shop that not only sells tea but a variety of other food that pairs perfectly with a cup of tea.

Our mission and vision

It took me a long time before I was able to open a tea shop of my own. In Tea Amoureux, our mission is to inspire and to spread the love of tea to people all around the world. Our vision is to expand our business worldwide in the future.

TEA AMOUREUX motto

In Tea Amoureux, our customer is our main priority. We are always working hard to make sure our customers are always satisfied and happy. We use only the best ingredients to make the tea because we want our customer to drink the best tea they have ever drank. We go by the slogan "Customer satisfication is our priority".
